Projects

BestPrices

Under continuous development whenever I have free time.

A personal idea to readily make grocery prices available to consumers. Users can search the database for an item found at the market and get info on its pricing at stores where data has been recorded.

My first project utilizing Django to get a basic understanding of its front/back end capabilities. I also developed the project within a docker container to practice utilizing a non-local environment for development.

Utilizing - Docker, Django, Heroku, PostgreSQL

Porthubolio

A web-app created to serve as a sort of fusion between Google Docs/Github and LinkedIn. Users fall into either a professional which is someone showcasing theirself and their portfolio, or a recruiter who can search through all professionals and reach out to those of interest.

Created by myself and two others in a group for a software development course. The premise was to familiarize working with a group on a larger scale project. We also were introduced to AGILE software development with weekly scrums to check our sprint achievements.

Utilizing - Firebase Realtime Database, Node.js, React, Redux

COVID-19 World Map Tracker

This web-app showcases a live portrayal of the world map in terms of COVID-19 statistics. A total world count of daily new cases, recoveries, and deaths. It also allows the user to check out the specifics of a certain country and get visual information of that countries statisics.

This project was created as part of a rapid prototyping course as my personal project of choice. It was my first web-app created that didn't rely on just plain JavaScript.

Utilizing - Chart.js, Disease.sh API, Firebase, Leaflet, React
